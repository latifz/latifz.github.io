Project Story

A robot teaching coding to smaller robots

  • Teacher enters the class.
  • Students enter the class one by one, greeting the teacher in their own way.
  • The teacher begins lecture on python.
  • She explains two slides.
  • As she's giving the lecture camera pans out to students.
  • One of the student asks a question.
  • The teacher explains the answer.
  • The student is satisfied with the answer. And the lecture is fast forwarded.
  • Lecture ends.
COMPONENTS: Characters (students and teacher) Props (blackboard/projector, desks, chairs Scene (classroom, lighting)
